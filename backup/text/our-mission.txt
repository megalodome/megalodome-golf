MEGALODOME GOLF'S UNIQUE EXPERIENCE:
Weathered Climate all Year-Round
Our initial focus is on extending the golf season in colder climates,
transforming a short 3- to 6-month golf period to a full 12-month.
However, with average temperatures steadily rising over the past decade,
we see a significant opportunity to enable year-round play in warmer regions as well
(where temperatures frequently exceed 90°F and can reach up to 130°F in extreme cases.)
Arizona Style Golf Course
Unique, fun and immersive experience for all ages and all levels:
At our first location in Chicago, golfers will feel as if they've been transported to Scottsdale, AZ —
even in the heart of winter! For the first time, a true golf
experience is available in a cutting-edge, fully immersive indoor environment.
Shorter Rounds
Shortening the amount of time required to play.  The average 9 hole round is expected to take only 90 minutes in our domes so a full 18 holes will only take about 3 hours (instead of 5 hours)
Strengthening Business Relationships
Creating an inspirational setting that will facilitate the development of business relationships & promote social interactions
Our advanced artificial greens are designed to replicate the performance of natural grass, allowing players to generate spin on approach shots just as they would on an outdoor course.
Colleges, universities, and sport-study programs will greatly benefit from our state-of-the-art facilities.
Reducing Maintenance Costs
Our maintenance expenses are significantly lower than those of a traditional golf course, as we eliminate the need for mowing, upkeep, and fertilization. This not only makes the game more affordable but also enhances our environmental sustainability.
Enhancing safety with strategically placed protective nets to ensure a secure environment for all golfers.

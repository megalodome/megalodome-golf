Introduction to MEGALODOME GOLF
The world’s first indoor golf experience that replicates “traditional golf course”
We offer an innovative and immersive golfing experience that allows players to enjoy the sport year-round (regardless of weather conditions). Our state-of-the-art facilities feature a meticulously designed golf course that provides a truly exceptional & fun golfing experience for players of all skill levels.  Our executive 9-hole course is a par 30 and is comprised of:
Six (6) par 3 holes
Three (3) par 4 holes
Par 3 Holes
Multifaceted Practice Facility
Par 3 Holes
The lengths of the par 3s will vary from 100 to 160 yards, including undulating greens well protected by bunkers and water hazards. Golf course design prepared by renowned golf architects Huxham Golf Design Inc.
Par 4 Holes
Multifaceted Practice Facility
Par 3 Holes
The Arizona-style golf course will feature three par-4 holes, each measuring between 270 and 280 yards. Strategically placed water hazards and bunkers will provide strong defenses, making it challenging to reach the green in a single stroke—an opportunity reserved for only the longest hitters.
Multifaceted Practice Facility
Multifaceted Practice Facility
Multifaceted Practice Facility
The fourth dome is a fully equipped practice facility featuring 50 stalls and extending over 275 yards.
Golfers can practice with every club in their bag while observing precise yardages and ball trajectories.
We will also have several stations with advanced simulator software.
Additionally, the short game area includes a chipping green with bunkers and a putting green, allowing players to refine both their short game and overall swing.
The short game area will include a chipping green with bunkers and a putting green enabling golfers to improve their short game in addition to their swing.

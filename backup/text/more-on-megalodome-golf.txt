MEGALODOME GOLF is also:
The World's First
The world’s first indoor golf experience to truly replicate “traditional golf” is set to revolutionize the sport.
This stunning Scottsdale, Arizona-style course is housed within the three largest domes in North America.
Experience
A stunning course featuring lakes, waste bunkers, and undulating greens, designed by renowned international golf architects.
Golfers will enjoy an elevated sensory experience, surrounded by the presence of cacti, palm trees, and four distinct types of artificial turf throughout the course.
Our Course
This executive 9-hole golf course features 6 par-3 holes and 3 par-4 holes, with a total par of 30.
The fourth dome serves as a full practice facility, offering 50 driving stalls and extending over 275 yards. Golfers will be able to practice with every club in their bag while being able to track exact yardages and ball trajectories. The facility will also include chipping greens with sand traps and a putting green, allowing for year-round practice of the full golf game—365 days a year!
This venue will be ideal for colleges, universities, and sport-study programs, offering a place to enhance their skills all year long.
Imagine the Possibilities
All aspects of the game of golf can be practiced year-round in a controlled, ideal temperature.
Imagine being able to hone your skills in your favorite sport, year-round, under perfect conditions from 6:00 AM to midnight.

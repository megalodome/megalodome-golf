Frequently Asked Questions

[email protected]
if you cannot find the answer to your question.
When MEGALODOME GOLF will be open?
We are in the financing process, our objective is to open at the fall of 2027.
Where will be your first location?
In Oswego, IL
What will be the cost to play a nine hole?
Our pricing is currently not finalized and will vary by season.
What are the operating hours of MEGALODOME GOLF?
We will be open from 6am-midnight Central Time, every day.
Is there a pro shop at MEGALODOME GOLF?
Yes, we have a fully stocked pro shop offering a wide selection of equipment for both rental and purchase (including left- and right-handed options), along with apparel for men and women and a variety of accessories.
Does MEGALODOME GOLF have a dress code?
Men: A collared shirt paired with shorts or slacks and spikeless golf shoes (no sneakers).
Women: Tailored shorts or pants with a blouse, polo shirt, or casual top, or golf attire such as a golf skirt and polo shirt. White denim pants are also acceptable for women. Spikeless golf shoes are required.
